Shoulders:
- Deltoids -- to rotate and lift the arm
  - Presses
    - machine presses
    - Arnold presses
    - incline barbell presses
    - dumbbell presses
    - military presses
    - machine presses
    - push presses
    - clean and presses
  - Raises
    - front dumbbell raises
    - front barbell presses
    - dumbbell lateral raises
    - cable lateral raises
- Trapezius (traps) -- to lift the entire shoulder girdle
  - barbell shrugs
  - upright rows
  - T-bar rows
  - cable rows


Chest:
- pectorals (pecs) -- to pull the arm and shoulder across the front of the body
  - Barbell flat bench presses
  - barbell incline bench presses
  - dumbbell flat presses
  - dumbbell incline Presses
  - dumbbell decline Presses
  - parallel bar dips
  - machine Presses
  - dumbbell fly's
  - standing cable crossovers
  - machine fly's
  - straight-arm pullovers
  -
- subclavius -- to draw the shoulder forward
- serratus anterior -- to rotate the scapula, raising the point of the shoulder and drawing the scapular forward and downward
   - rope pulls
   - one-arm cable pulls
   - machine pullovers
   - hanging serratus crunch

Back:
- latissimus dorsi (lats) -- to pull the shoulders downward and to the Back
  - chin-ups
  - lat machine pulldowns
  - bent-over barbell rows
  - bent-over dumbbell rows
  - T-bar rows
  - one-arm dumbbell rows
  - seated cable rows

- spinal erectors -- to hold the spine erect
  - deadlifts
  - good mornings
  - hyperextensions

Arms:
- biceps brachii (biceps) -- to lift and curl the arm, to pronate the wrist
  - standing barbell curls
  - preacher curls
  - 21's
  - incline dumbbell curls
  - hammer curls
  - concentration curls
  - two-hand cable curls
  - reverse curls
  - machine curls
- triceps brachii (triceps) -- to straighten the armand supinate the wrist
  - cable pressdowns
  - triceps Presses
  - lying triceps hyperextensions
  - dumbbell kickbacks
  - one-arm triceps extensions
  - dips
- forearm flexors -- to curl the palm down and forward
  - barbell wrist curls
- forearm extensors -- to curl the knuckles back and up`
  - barbell reverse wrist curls

Uppers Legs:
- quadriceps (quads) -- to extend and straighten the leg
  - barbell squats
  - machine squats
  - front squats
  - hack squats
  - leg Presses
  - lunges
  - leg extensions

- biceps femoris (hamstrings, hammies) -- to curl the leg back
  - leg curls
  - straight-leg deadlifts

- gluteus maximus (glutes)
- tensor fasciae latae
- rectus femoris
- vastus lateralis
- vastus medialis
- sartorius

Lower Legs (calves):
- soleus -- to flex the foot
- gastrocnemius -- to flex the foot
- tibialis anterior -- to flex the foot
  - standing calf Raises
  - saeted calf Raises
  - reverse calf raises

Abdomen:
- rectus abdominis (abs) -- to flex the spinal column and to draw the sternum toward the pelvis
  - roman chairs
  - crunches
  - twisting crunches
  - reverse crunches
  - hanging reverse crunches
  - vertical bench crunches
  - cable crunches
  - machine crunches
  - seated leg tucks
  - hanging leg raises
- external obliques (obliques) -- to flex and rotate the spinal column
  - seated twists
  - bent-over twists
- intercostals -- to lift the ribs and draw them together
  - side leg Raises
  - front kicks
